My goal is to preserve and promote heritage languages around the world. I believe that language is an integral part of cultural identity and heritage, and it deserves to be celebrated and preserved for future generations.

My Objectives:

  • To raise awareness about the importance of heritage language preservation.
  • To provide resources and support for heritage language learners.

My Vision:

  • A world where every heritage language is valued, celebrated, and preserved for generations to come.
  • A global community of diverse linguistic and cultural heritage thriving in harmony.
